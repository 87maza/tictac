Ticky Tacky

Rules:

The objective of Tic Tac Toe is to get three in a row.

  • Player 1 choses and icon and Player 2/Computer choses one.
  • Players take turns placing their icons on the game board until either opponent has three in a row or all nine squares are filled.
  • Computer/Player 2 always goes first, and in the event that no one has three in a row, the stalemate is called a cat's game.
